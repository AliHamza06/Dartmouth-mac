Dartmouth Blockchain is a community of builders, thinkers, and innovators.

We are an ecosystem for committed, talented individuals to build their blockchain competency and connect with other students interested in blockchain technology at Dartmouth.

We conduct academic research on privacy solutions, new financial primitives, MEV, and smart contract security with the goal of resolving the issues necessary to scale blockchain technology to millions of users.

We build solutions to problems in the blockchain ecosystem with the goal of making blockchain topics more understandable and accessible to other university students around the globe.

We invest in ideas and projects that are fundamentally sound and give our members an opportunity to dive deeper into and support the blockchain space.
